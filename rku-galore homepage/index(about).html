Since 2007, Galore has been a celebration of talent and teamwork, bringing together over 10,000 students from 18 Indian states and 35 countries. With 9 exciting sports including Cricket, Volleyball, Football, Basketball, Dodgeball, Carrom, Chess, Badminton, Table-Tennis and 6 cultural events spanning Digital Photo Competition, Artistry - Drawing competition, Singing Competition, Public Speaking Contest, Rangoli Competition, Solo Dance Competition, Duo Dance Competition and Group Dance Competition. it showcases the dedication and creativity cultivated throughout the year.

Galore is a vibrant blend of excellence, diversity, and camaraderie. Beyond celebrating achievements, it fosters inclusivity, sportsmanship, and a sense of unity, making it one of the most eagerly awaited events of the year.

Registration is mandatory for participation in all events.
